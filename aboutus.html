HOW DID WE START?

Our team consists of two undergraduate students. They both attended Leeds International School and finished their A Levels in Edexcel Stream. Both of them acquired a significant amount of their coding and media skills while attending NSBM Green University.

NSBM Green Universiry is the South Asia's top institution for giving degrees, NSBM Green University, is the pioneer in its field. It is a government-owned, independently sponsored institution that is under the Ministry of Education's jurisdiction. As the top educational institution in the nation, NSBM has developed into a highly responsible higher education institution that promotes sustainable living while providing exceptional possibilities and holistic education that meets worldwide standards.

Advance Facilities

Five faculties make up NSBM's extensive list of undergraduate and graduate degree programs: Business, Computing, Engineering, Science, and Postgraduate & Professional Advancement. The study programs offered at NSBM are either in-house courses approved by the Ministry of Higher Education and the University Grants Commission, or they are top-tier international courses offered in collaboration with prestigious foreign universities like Victoria University in Australia and the University of Plymouth in the United Kingdom.

Details

NSBM nurtures its graduates to become productive members of society with their specialization ranging in study fields such as business, management, computing, IT, engineering, science, psychology, nursing, interior design, quantity surveying, law, and multimedia. Focused on producing competent professionals and innovative entrepreneurs for the increasingly globalized world, NSBM nurtures its graduates. With a goal of becoming recognized worldwide and becoming the greatest graduate school in Sri Lanka, NSBM now enrolls about 3000 new students annually and has more than 11,000 residents who are enrolled in more than 50 degree programs across 4 faculties.

Additionally, NSBM Green University has given the country 14,000+ graduates over the years and demonstrated its worldwide presence by building a network of alumni around the globe. The NSBM Green University is an ultra-modern university complex built with cutting-edge amenities that offers the ideal setting for top-notch teaching, learning, and research. It is located on a 40-acre plot of land in Pitipana, Homagama, amidst the tranquility and greenery.
